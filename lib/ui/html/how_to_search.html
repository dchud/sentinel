How to Search the Canary Database

During the initial development of the Canary Database, curated studies can be searched by:

  • Keyword: matches when all terms are found in any of the title, authors, or abstracts of curated studies.
  • Author: matches author names in the form "Lastname FM", where 'F' and 'M' are first and middle initials; this is adopted directly from MEDLINE convention. For best results, search on the last name only.
  • Title: matches terms found only in the titles of curated studies.

We are currently developing the database model and indexing strategies for the Canary Database. Because the studies we curate will include information from a variety of abstracting and indexing sources, we are exploring ways to make advanced searching capabilities easy to use across these source records. We expect to offer a flexible advanced search feature soon.

You can also browse the database by year, journal, study type, or by subject heading.
